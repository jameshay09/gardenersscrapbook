Ingredients

Method

Comments

  • 3 to 4 lbs of apples (about 7 to 10 apples, depending on the size), peeled, cored, and quartered*
  • 2 strips of lemon peel (use a vegetable peeler to strip 4 lengths, zest only, not the pith)
  • 3 Tbsp lemon juice or apple cider vinegar (more or less to taste)
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • Up to 1/2 cup of white sugar (can half of the white sugar with brown sugar)
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
