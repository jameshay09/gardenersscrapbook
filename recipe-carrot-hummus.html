Ingredients

Method

Comments

  1. 1 tablespoon caraway seeds
  2. 1 tablespoon coriander seeds
  3. 1 tablespoon cumin seeds
  4. 6 tablespoons olive oil, divided, plus more for drizzling
  5. ½ pound carrots, chopped
  6. Kosher salt, freshly ground pepper
  7. 1 15-ounce can chickpeas, drained
  8. 2 garlic cloves, finely grated
  9. ½ cup carrot juice or water
  10. ¼ cup natural smooth peanut butter
  11. 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  12. ¼ cup unsalted, roasted peanuts
  13. 1 tablespoon honey
  14. 2 canned chipotle chiles in adobo, plus 2 tablespoons adobo sauce

 

 

 

 

 

Preheat the oven to 200C/400C/gas mark 6. Cut the carrots into bite-size pieces, put them on a baking tray and roast them in the oven for 25 minutes.

 

Drain the chickpeas and rinse thoroughly. Peel and finely chop the garlic. Put all the ingredients into a blender and blend until smooth, adding a little water if necessary. Check the seasoning and add more salt and pepper if necessary.
